About Me

Hi there! I'm Iryna Karmazinova-Honcharenko, a passionate Graphic Designer, UI/UX Designer, and Illustrator. With a creative vision and an eye for detail, I specialize in crafting visually appealing designs that effectively communicate ideas and tell stories.
